About Us: "Since 1881"

The Medford, MA chapter of the American Red Cross is a sub-set of the largest and most diverse service organization in the United States. We are affiliated with the larger American Red Cross Massachusetts Region, which serves the 7.029 million residents of the 351 cities and towns of Massachusetts.
